Your Privacy Matters

This Privacy Policy explains how Under Construction Expedition (createmodfan12) collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our educational services.

1 Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us and information that is automatically collected when you use our services.

Information You Provide:

  • Name and contact information
  • Educational institution details
  • Purchase and payment information
  • Communication preferences
  • Feedback and survey responses

Automatically Collected:

  • Usage data and analytics
  • Device and browser information
  • IP address and location data
  • Cookies and tracking technologies
  • Performance and error logs

2 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our educational services:

Service Delivery

  • • Process purchases and payments
  • • Deliver educational materials
  • • Provide customer support
  • • Manage user accounts

Communication

  • • Send important updates
  • • Respond to inquiries
  • • Share educational content
  • • Conduct surveys

Improvement

  • • Analyze usage patterns
  • • Enhance user experience
  • • Develop new features
  • • Ensure security

3 Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

With Your Consent

We may share information when you explicitly agree to such sharing.

Service Providers

Trusted third parties who assist in operating our services (payment processors, hosting providers).

Legal Requirements

When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety.

4 Data Security and Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Security Measures:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure data transmission protocols
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Employee training on data protection

Important Note:

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to using industry-standard practices.

5 Your Privacy Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal information. Depending on your location, these may include:

Access

Request a copy of your personal data

Correction

Update or correct inaccurate information

Deletion

Request removal of your personal data

Portability

Receive your data in a portable format

Opt-out

Unsubscribe from marketing communications

Restriction

Limit how we process your information

6 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and analyze usage patterns.

Types of Cookies We Use:

Essential Cookies

Required for basic site functionality

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand site usage

Preference Cookies

Remember your settings and choices

7 Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

Retention Periods:

  • • Account information: Until account deletion or 3 years of inactivity
  • • Purchase records: 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • • Usage analytics: 2 years for service improvement
  • • Marketing data: Until you opt-out or 2 years of inactivity

8 Children's Privacy

The Under Construction Expedition is designed for educational use and may be used by minors under adult supervision. We are committed to protecting children's privacy.

Special Protections for Children:

  • • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent
  • • Educational institutions must ensure appropriate consent for student use
  • • Parents and guardians can request access to or deletion of their child's information
  • • We comply with applicable children's privacy laws (COPPA, GDPR-K, etc.)

9 Contact Us About Privacy

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your privacy rights, please contact us.
